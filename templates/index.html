What this tool does

Buying a home for the first time means hearing a lot of new words and dealing with a lot of new people — lenders, agents, escrow officers, inspectors. This tool breaks all of it down one step at a time, with answers focused on Riverside County — things like FHA loans, CalHFA down payment help, and what homes actually cost around Murrieta and Temecula.

It will not replace your lender or your agent. It helps you walk into those conversations already understanding what they're talking about.
